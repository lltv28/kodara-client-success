Strategy & Implementation

Pricing Philosophy

Prices reflect the value of the outcome delivered, not the cost to produce or the number of minutes of content. A 15-minute diagnostic that gives someone instant clarity on their #1 vocal problem is worth more than a 3-hour lecture that leaves them confused.

Price Tiers by Strategic Role

  • Liquidators ($17): #2, #9, #20 — impulse-buy entry points that offset ad spend and convert strangers into buyers
  • Tripwires ($27–$67): #1, #3, #7, #8, #13, #15, #17, #18, #19 — build trust, demonstrate expertise, create momentum toward core offers
  • Upsell Triggers ($47–$297): #5, #6, #10, #11, #14, #16 — solve a problem that reveals a bigger problem, creating desire for the core offer

The $297 Price Anchor

#14 (The Diagnostic Ear) at $297 creates a brilliant strategic anchor. A teacher who invests $297 in learning the diagnostic framework has already committed to the approach. The AI Studio Assistant at $397 is only $100 more — and it automates the framework they just learned to value. This is the most powerful upsell path in the entire product line.

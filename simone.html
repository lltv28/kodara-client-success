01 — The Pain

You’re not burned out because you work too much

You’re burned out because you’re still running your business like it’s day one—and every major decision still runs through you.

Still the bottleneck

  • You have a team, clients, and revenue—but every major decision still runs through you
  • Every fire, you’re the one putting it out
  • Every plan, you’re the one pushing it across the finish line

Lack of structure

  • 93% of employees don’t understand how their work connects to company strategy (Harvard)
  • When your team doesn’t see the big picture, they can’t make decisions—so they keep coming back to you
  • Working 80–90 hours a week, not getting paid what you should, and the business still can’t run without you

No time to document

  • You’ve read the books (E-Myth, Traction, Good to Great)—you know you need systems
  • You’ve tried templates, courses, consultants—they all require YOU to do the heavy lifting
  • Every quarter you delay, you’re leaving money on the table and burning out faster

“You just hired someone who seems great, but two weeks in they’re asking you 47 questions a day because nothing is documented, and you realize you’re spending more time managing them than you were just doing the work yourself—and you’re about to lose your mind.”

02 — The Solution

8 capabilities, zero manual work

Bulletproof COO AI observes your business, then builds your entire operating system—so you don’t have to.

Observe & Plan

  • Observes and learns your business for 1–2 weeks by analyzing your calendar, emails, Slack messages, meeting recordings, existing docs, and short voice memos where you brain-dump what’s in your head
  • Generates a complete company VVMMOO (Vision, Values, Methods, Measures, Obstacles, Opportunities) based on what you’re actually doing and saying—not what you wish you were doing
  • Creates department-level VVMMOOs for each business area that reflect how your business actually operates, with role-specific methods and measures pulled from real work patterns
  • Writes individual contributor VVMMOOs for every team member based on their current responsibilities, communication patterns, and how they actually spend their time

Execute & Scale

  • Builds a 13-week action plan with weekly priorities and pre-filled WAR meeting agendas by analyzing what’s already on your roadmap, in your CRM, and in your team’s task lists
  • Produces one-on-one prep forms and coaching prompts for each direct report by identifying recurring issues, growth opportunities, and communication gaps from your actual interactions
  • Generates onboarding plans for new hires by documenting how your current team was trained (pulled from emails, recordings, and existing processes)
  • Creates delegation frameworks by identifying what’s currently on your plate that could be owned by someone else, then writes the handoff plan with decision-making parameters

03 — Differentiation

Why this isn’t “just another AI tool”

Generic AI says…

  • Create a strategic plan for your business
  • Delegate more tasks to your team
  • Set up weekly team meetings
  • Document your processes
  • Use project management software

Simone’s AI says…

  • You don’t train it—it trains itself by studying your business like a world-class COO would during their first two weeks on the job
  • Trained on Simone’s VVMMOO methodology, so it knows the difference between what you’re doing (often firefighting) and what you should be doing (strategic work), and it builds the plan to close that gap
  • Understands the nuance of delegation (three levels, five C’s) and won’t just tell you to “delegate more”—it tells you exactly what to delegate, to whom, and how to hand it off without it coming back broken
  • Outputs are based on your actual business—not generic templates—it uses your language, your priorities, your team structure, and your real constraints
  • Includes Simone’s coaching voice to challenge you when you’re holding onto work that isn’t yours (e.g., “Is this really yours to do?” “What would a $30M CEO do here?”)

Manual alternative

  • Hiring a COO or integrator: $80K–$150K/year, plus 3–6 months to onboard, and they still need you to document everything first
  • DIY with templates: 40+ hours of work you don’t have, and the plan is outdated the moment you finish it because your business keeps moving
  • Hiring a consultant: $10K–$20K and 3–6 months of meetings where you’re still doing most of the thinking and documentation
  • Trying to get your team to document processes: they don’t know what you know, so you end up rewriting everything anyway

06 — Package Details

What’s included—and what’s not

Core Product

Bulletproof COO AI is a done-for-you business operating system that studies your business for 1–2 weeks, then generates your strategic plan, delegation frameworks, meeting structures, and onboarding systems—plus the training and templates you need to implement it all immediately. Buyers walk away with a complete, customized operating system (VVMMOO, WAR meetings, delegation plans, onboarding docs) built specifically for their business, ready to use, and backed by 10+ years of proven methodology.

Exclusions

  • Full Bulletproof Business Builder program or mastermind access—would cannibalize the $3K–$15K ongoing programs
  • One-on-one advisory or coaching calls—keeps the product scalable and digital
  • Advanced hiring frameworks or team optimization modules—too broad, would bloat the product

6 Inclusions

VVMMOO Masterclass

Video training (60–90 minutes): walks through the methodology behind the AI so buyers understand what the AI is building and why it matters.

WAR Meeting Toolkit

Templates + 15-minute video: pre-built agendas, scoreboard templates, and facilitation guide.

Delegation Decision Tree

One-page framework + examples: visual guide to the three levels of delegation and five C’s.

CEO Time Audit Template

Spreadsheet + 10-minute walkthrough video: helps buyers track where their time currently goes.

Onboarding SOP Library

5–10 example documents: real onboarding plans, training checklists, and 30/60/90-day goal templates.

Private Slack Channel or Monthly Group Q&A

90 days of access: founding members get 90 days to ask questions, troubleshoot, and share wins.
